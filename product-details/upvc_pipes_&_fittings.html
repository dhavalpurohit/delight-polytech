UPVC Pipes

Delight UPVC Pipes & Fittings are synthetic plastic polymer made from most versatile polymer called Un-Plasticized Polyvinyl Chloride

UPVC pipes & fittings have special blend of formulation in order to give remarkable flexibility.

Exceptionally, tendency of lesser cost, minimal maintenance and higher water transfer efficiency have made Delight pipes & fittings as a perfect replacement to cast iron plumbing pipes & fittings. Delight Exim uPVC Pipes are available from size 1/2″ to 3″ in both SCH-40 & SCH-80 and fittings from size 1/2″ to 3″ in SCH-80.
