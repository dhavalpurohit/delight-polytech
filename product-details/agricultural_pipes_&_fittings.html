Agricultural Pipes & Fitting

We offer agriculture pvc pipes and fittings. The Self fit (Solvent cement Joint) pipes have one end self-socketed and the other end plain and elastomeric seal ring fit pipes have sealing ring joints, which fits snugly without the use of couples. The strong solvent cement joint and sealing sing joints are permanent and trouble free.

Advantage:
  1. Selffit sockets and sealing ring joints are formed with high precision on modernized developed machines.
  2. 50% saving in installation time, as compared with plain ended pipes and loose couplers.
  3. The number joints are reduced by 50%, resulting in substantial saving in labor costs.
  4. liminates the inconvenience of loose couplers, & reduction in inventory costs.
  5. Selfit and sealing ring joint pipes are supplied in a standard length of 6 mtrs. Exclusive of the socket portion.
